About Me

My journey with technology started back home in India, where I earned my Bachelor's in Information Technology from Somaiya University. That's where I discovered my love for data analysis, data engineering, and software development—basically, anything that lets me turn raw data into something meaningful.

Wanting to push myself further, I took a big leap and moved to the U.S. to pursue my Master's in Computer Science at Stevens Institute of Technology. Along the way, I've worked as a Data Analyst, diving deep into Python, MySQL, and machine learning, solving real-world problems, and collaborating with teams to make sense of complex data.

I've built projects that I'm really proud of like a clothing recommendation system and a stock market prediction model because I love creating solutions that actually make an impact. Now based in Union City, NJ, I'm constantly learning, growing, and looking for exciting opportunities to blend technology, creativity, and problem-solving to make a difference.
